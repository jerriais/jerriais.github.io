Penetanguishene, Ontario, Canada
Thursday, July 16, 2009

Great family camping weekend of campfires (i think we must have burned the equivalent of a small forest), frisbee, swimming, volleyball, canoeing, hiking. And the main event of the weekend: eating.

Some of us camped over night (me, Matt, Anice, Piyali, Calvin, Aunty Ivy, Uncle Hong). And others came for the day (Amy, Winnie, Uncle Francis, Aunty Judy, Grandma Wong, Uncle Kee, Aunty Kit, May, Kitty, Kinson).

Despite a few cloudy/rainy moments, it was delightful. I believe I gorged myself to a new level, something I don't aspire to do again.
