St. helier, United Kingdom
Saturday, August 2, 2008

The Gods were smiling upon us, we made it onto the ferry.

We changed our plans slightly and spent the month of August here.

During that time, we sold Foxy to a young South African couple living in London. They plan to fix her up and take her down the continent next year.

Full circle ending. Aren't those nice?
