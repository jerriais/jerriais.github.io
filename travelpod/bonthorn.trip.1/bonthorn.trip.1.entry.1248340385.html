Markham, Ontario, Canada
Wednesday, July 15, 2009

The day started with taking May and Grandma Wong for a spin on the Shadow. They loved it!

Then it was off to a good dimsum meal with all our grandparents.

And then began the mahjong marathon. Matt is getting to be a really good MJ player. And although I know the basics of the game, he is definitely more into it than me, and it's a great way to spend time with my grandmothers. Who wouldn't want a 'gweilo' grandson-in-law to give you money at a game you so enjoy winning?

And then before we know it, it's dinnertime! So back we go to the same restaurant for dinner.

What a day!
