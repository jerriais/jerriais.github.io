Chalabre, Languedoc-Roussillon, France
Thursday, July 17, 2008

These guys are fit. That's all i need to say.

We biked the 30 or so km roundtrip to Chalabre to catch the cyclists whizzing by, but i was SO pathetic going up a the hill. No Tour for me, thanks, i'll just watch by the sidelines and eat my chocolate croissant.
